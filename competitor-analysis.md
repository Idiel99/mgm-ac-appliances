## Key Takeaway

MGM has the **best design and tech foundation** of any competitor analyzed. The site looks more modern and loads faster than all 3 competitors. However, the competitors win on **content depth and trust signals** — reviews, offers, licensing, financing, and multiple pages of service information. 

The biggest quick wins are: **real testimonials, a working contact form, special offers, and license display**. These are table-stakes that every competitor has and that potential customers expect to see.
